<h4>The firm and market structures: Summary table</h4>
<br>

||Perfect competition|Monopolistic competition|Oligopoly|Monopoly|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|# of Sellers|Many|Many|Few|Single|
|Barries to entry|Very low|Low|High|Very High|
